Featured Upcoming Events

HALF CENTURY CLUB LUNCHEON - Wednesday, February 18

MD graduates from the Class of 1964 and earlier are invited to an exclusive luncheon in honor of all medical alumni who graduated more than 50 years ago.

This year's event will feature Philip A. Pizzo, MD, former dean of the Stanford University School of Medicine and Founding Director of the Stanford Distinguished Careers Institute.

Read more

MEDICAL MATCHES: A Market Design Perspective - Wednesday, March 4

Join the Stanford University Medical Center Alumni Association for an evening with Nobel Prize Winner Alvin Roth, MS ’73, PhD ’74, as he explains how he uses economic theory and marketplace algorithms to systemize courtship in real world settings. From redesigning the National Residency Matching Program to helping surgeons maximize transplant exchange programs, he will share how the economics of transactions help us find new ways of connecting.
