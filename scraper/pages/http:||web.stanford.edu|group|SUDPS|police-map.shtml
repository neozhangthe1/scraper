The Stanford University Department of Public Safety is located at 711 Serra Street
between Campus Drive East and El Camino Real, near the gas station and Maples Pavilion.


From the 101 Fwy: Exit University Ave. towards Palo Alto/Stanford University. Turn left on El Camino Real followed by a right on Serra St. The police dept will be on your right-hand side after the first stop sign.

From the 280 Fwy: Exit Pagemill Rd. heading east. Turn left on El Camino Real followed by another left on Serra St. The police dept will be on your right-hand side after the first stop sign.
